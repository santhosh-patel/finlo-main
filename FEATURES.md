# Finlo: The Ultimate Personal Ledger

Finlo is a premium, AI-powered personal finance management application designed for precision, speed, and deep financial insight. Below is a detailed breakdown of every feature and functionality available in the app.

---

## 1. Core Transaction Management

The heart of Finlo is its extremely fast and intuitive transaction logging system.

*   **Expense & Income Tracking**: Record every outflow and inflow with precision.
*   **Multi-Currency Support**: Log transactions in INR, USD, EUR, or GBP. The app handles FX rates automatically.
*   **Categorization**: Organise spending into top-level categories (e.g., Food, Travel) and detailed subcategories.
*   **Receipt Scanning**: Upload or take a photo of a receipt, and Finlo's AI will automatically extract the amount, merchant, and date.
*   **Rich Metadata**: Add notes, payment methods (UPI, Cash, Card), and tags to any transaction.
*   **Soft Delete (Trash Bin)**: Expenses are never permanently deleted immediately. They move to a Trash Bin where they can be restored within 7 days.
*   **Reimbursable Expenses**: Mark transactions as "Reimbursable" (e.g., for work) and track when they have been settled.

---

## 2. Artificial Intelligence (Maya AI)

Finlo features **Maya**, a state-of-the-art AI assistant that helps you manage your money through natural language.

*   **Natural Language Logging**: Type or speak "I spent 500 on dinner at Olive yesterday," and Maya will parse it into a structured transaction instantly.
*   **Ask Maya**: A dedicated chat interface where you can ask complex questions about your data:
    *   *"How much did I spend on coffee last month?"*
    *   *"What is my biggest expense category this week?"*
    *   *"Am I overspending on subscription services?"*
*   **Voice HUD**: A hands-free mode for logging transactions using your voice.
*   **Spending Insights**: Maya periodically analyzes your spending habits to provide personalized advice and identify anomalies (e.g., a unusually high bill).

---

## 3. Subscription Management

Never lose track of your recurring payments or "hidden" SaaS costs.

*   **Subscription Detection**: Finlo automatically analyzes your transaction history to find recurring patterns and identifies potential subscriptions.
*   **Manual Subscriptions**: Manually track services like Netflix, Apple Music, or Gym memberships.
*   **Billing Cycles**: Support for Weekly, Monthly, and Yearly cycles.
*   **Smart Alerts**: Set custom lead times (e.g., "Alert me 2 days before") to receive a notification in the app before a subscription is billed.
*   **Next Bill Tracker**: See exactly how many days are left until your next bill is due.

---

## 4. Advanced Lending & Borrowing

Track money lent to friends or borrowed from others with full transaction history.

*   **Lent vs. Borrowed**: Clear separation of money owed to you and money you owe.
*   **Partial Repayments**: Record individual payments against a loan principal over time.
*   **Payment History**: Every loan features a detailed ledger showing all repayment transactions.
*   **Interest Support**: Add Flat or Simple interest rates to any loan.
*   **Progress Bars**: Visual indicators showing exactly how much of a loan has been repaid.
*   **Due Dates**: Set optional due dates and get reminders when they approach.

---

## 5. Budgeting & Planning

Stay disciplined with per-category monthly limits.

*   **Category Budgets**: Set specific monthly spending caps for each category.
*   **Safe to Spend**: A dynamic calculation that tells you exactly how much "surplus" cash you have left for the month based on your current spending and active budgets.
*   **Budget Alerts**: Visual warnings and notifications when you reach 80% or 100% of a category's budget.
*   **Historical Analysis**: Compare your current spending against previous months to track progress.

---

## 6. Visualization & Views

Understand your finances through multiple lenses.

*   **Today View**: A focused look at the current day's activity.
*   **Weekly View**: A bar chart and list view of your spending throughout the week.
*   **Monthly View**: A high-level summary of your monthly performance, including income vs. expense balance.
*   **Interactive Charts**: Dynamic visualizations in the AI chat for deeper data analysis.

---

## 7. Data Portability & Sync

Your data belongs to you. Finlo ensures it's always accessible and portable.

*   **Supabase Cloud Sync**: Real-time synchronization across all your devices (Web, Mobile, Desktop).
*   **Offline Mode**: Log transactions even without internet. They will automatically sync when you're back online.
*   **Import CSV/Excel**: Easily migrate your data from other apps or bank statements.
*   **Export (JSON/CSV)**: Download full backups of your data at any time for your own records or for use in spreadsheets.
*   **Data Restoration**: Restore your ledger from a previous backup with "Merge" or "Replace" modes.

---

## 8. Premium Experience

Designed for those who appreciate fine details and high performance.

*   **Glassmorphic Design**: A stunning, modern UI with blurred backgrounds, smooth gradients, and premium typography.
*   **Dark & Light Modes**: Full support for system-wide themes.
*   **Micro-Animations**: Fluid transitions and haptic feedback (on mobile) for a satisfying user experience.
*   **Privacy First**: Secure authentication via Supabase and granular Role-Based Access Control.
*   **Admin Tools**: Built-in support for impersonation (for debugging) and audit logs to ensure system integrity.
